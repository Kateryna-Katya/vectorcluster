About the Game

It's basically a fantasy puzzle game
where you're constantly exploring and finding new stuff

Strongblade mixes classic match-3 puzzles with storytelling and lets you rebuild the world as you play.
Players solve colorful puzzle boards to unlock hidden areas, repair villages, discover treasures, and continue their expedition through magical lands.
We want the game to feel fun and rewarding without being too heavy, but still give you that explorer's rush. 

How to Play

Solve puzzles and
uncover new regions

Match Magical Symbols

Create combinations to clear objectives and progress through levels.

Use Adventure Boosters

Activate bombs, magic effects, and powerful combo abilities.

Explore Fantasy Locations

Travel through forests, ruins, villages, and hidden caves.

Complete Story Missions

Help characters overcome obstacles and reveal ancient mysteries.

Restore the World

Repair damaged locations and unlock decorative improvements.
